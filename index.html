San Francisco, officially City and County of San Francisco and colloquially known by its initialism SF, is a city in, and the cultural, commercial, and financial center of, Northern California. San Francisco is the 4th most populous city in California, and the 13th most populous in the United States. San Francisco started as a mission in 1776 and was incorporated in 1850.
